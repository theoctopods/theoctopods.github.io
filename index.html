How To Mint

  1. BUY SOL FROM AN EXCHANGE

    Buy SOL (Solana) from your favorite crypto exchange. If you plan on converting another crypto into SOL, the easiest way is still using an exchange. Here are a few suggestions:

    USA buyers:

      Binance.us
      Coinbase
      FTX.us
      Gate.io
      Kraken

    GLOBAL buyers:

      Binance
      Bitfinex
      FTX
      Gate.io
      Huobi

  2. DOWNLOAD AND INSTALL A SOL WALLET

    Download a Solana wallet

      For Desktop/Laptop, use Phantom on your browser.
      For Mobile, use Solflare

  3. TRANSFER FROM EXCHANGE TO WALLET

    Transfer funds from your exchange to your wallet. Always transfer a bit more than what you plan on spending to pay for gas. Solana is practically free but there is still a small gas fee. We recommend you transfer an additional +0.02 SOL per NFT to pay for minting fees. Yes, they are that low on Solana.


  4. MINT YOUR NFT

    Mint your Octopods NFT on our website through the mint button. Mint button will be available soon.
